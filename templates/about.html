About Us

Improving Lives Through Learning

Our Internship Program allows candidates the unique opportunity to achieve a valuable work experience in almost any industry across New Zealand. It is designed both for those completing an Internship as part of their study (for which you will receive academic credits), or for those wishing to gain work exposure in their field of interest.

An Internship is an excellent way to gain the skills and experience required to launch your career. All placements are initial un-paid positions, but might turn into employment. They are available at any time of the year and their duration, location and other specifics are left entirely up to you.
